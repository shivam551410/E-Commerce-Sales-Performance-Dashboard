# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## 📖 Project Overview

The **E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize key business metrics for an online retail company. It provides valuable insights into sales performance, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and regional sales trends.

This dashboard enables business managers and decision-makers to monitor KPIs, identify top-performing products and categories, evaluate payment preferences, and optimize business strategies using data-driven insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. Without proper visualization, it becomes difficult to identify profitable products, customer purchasing trends, regional performance, and sales opportunities.

This dashboard helps stakeholders monitor overall business performance and make informed decisions to improve sales and profitability.

# 📈 Business Insights

- Maharashtra generated the highest overall sales.
- Beauty and Electronics were the top-performing categories.
- UPI and Cash on Delivery were among the most preferred payment methods.
- Monthly sales peaked during March and April.
- A small group of customers contributed significantly to total revenue.
- Several products generated high sales but lower profit margins, indicating opportunities for pricing optimization.
